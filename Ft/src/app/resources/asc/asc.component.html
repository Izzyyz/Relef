Normal Skin

How can you identify your skin type?


Characteristics: Smooth and uniform texture, without excessive oiliness or noticeable dryness. Key products in the routine: Use gentle cleansers that maintain the skin's natural balance, refreshing toners, hydrating serums, and moisturizers that maintain hydration without adding excess oil.

Dry Skin

How can you identify your skin type?


Characteristics: Feeling of tightness and flakiness after washing your face, lack of natural glow. Key products in the routine: Look for products like gentle and creamy cleansers (such as moisturizing Cleansing Milk), hydrating toners, and creams with humectants like hyaluronic acid and rich emollients.

Oily skin

How can you identify your skin type?


Characteristics: Excessive shine in the T-zone (forehead, nose, chin), visible pores, prone to blackheads and pimples. Key products in the routine: Opt for cleansers that remove excess oil (like Deep Cleansing Foam), toners that control sebum production, serums with ingredients like tea tree, and lightweight or oil-free moisturizers.
